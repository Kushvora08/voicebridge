    About Voice Bridge

    Voice Bridge is a minimalist and powerful web-based translation tool designed for simplicity and speed. It leverages the cutting-edge capabilities of a large language model to provide high-quality translations in a wide variety of languages.

    Our goal is to make global communication effortless. With a clean interface and seamless user experience, Voice Bridge removes the complexities often associated with translation tools, allowing you to focus on your message.

    The translations are powered by the **gemini-2.5-flash-preview-05-20** model from Google, which excels at understanding and generating nuanced text across different languages.
